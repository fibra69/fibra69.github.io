About Us

The Romanian Orthodox community 'Saint Veronica' in Coventry aims to provide a place of prayer for Romanian Orthodox Christians in Coventry and the surrounding cities, as well as for any Christian who finds a place of prayer in our community.

You are all welcome.

Our Patron Saints

Saint Veronica

She who wiped the face of the Saviour

Saint Veronica is remembered by the Church as the woman who, moved by compassion, offered her veil to Our Lord Jesus Christ on the Way of the Cross. His holy face was miraculously imprinted upon the cloth.

St. Irodion of Lainici

Romanian saint canonised in 2017

Saint Irodion lived at Lainici Monastery in Gorj, Romania, devoted to prayer and monastic life. He is venerated by the Romanian Orthodox Church as a protector of the faithful and an intercessor before God.
